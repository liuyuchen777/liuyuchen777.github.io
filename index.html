Research Topic
My research is focused on adopting Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) to small-cell mobile communication system to optimize parameters like radio-wave, transmission power or any other possible factors which is potentially degrade the overall system performance due to inter-cell interference.
In present stage, we used Deep-Q-Learning multi-agent scheme to realize fair performance but fast integrated solution for transmission power optimization and 3D-beamforming vector joint optimization. The completely multi-agent training method and more optimization parameter will be discussed in the future research.
